What this dashboard will do
Compare two retrospective paths over the past X years using identical cash flows: Path A buys a home with the down payment and pays the mortgage monthly; Path B rents at the same monthly cost, invests the down payment as a lump sum into the chosen stock, and re-invests any monthly surplus (rent vs. mortgage) into the same stock. Subsequent steps will pull historical data for both housing (FHFA HPI) and equity (the chosen ticker), then net out interest, principal, equity build, and portfolio value.
